### Notes

- Best first-wave positioning: "dice-crafting roguelike" and "Balatro x Yahtzee" are the clearest hooks.
- The safest outreach targets are creators who already cover either Dice A Million directly or highly adjacent games such as Balatro, Dicey Dungeons, and Luck be a Landlord.
